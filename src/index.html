The project provides you with an easy way to share your data with community science for free.

 

Sign up
  Login  

VAO OpenID account is required to use the system.

It's easy to share your data: drag-and-drop files to the browser window and we will recognize the filetype, extract what we can from your files and if we find a table - create a database you can query.

Share your data with scientific community by providing URLs that everyone can browse. Create a writeable share for your workgroup to work with data together.
